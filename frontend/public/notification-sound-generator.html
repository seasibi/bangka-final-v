Notification Sound Generator

This page generates a simple notification beep sound that you can download.

Note: Since we can't generate MP3 directly in the browser, you'll need to:

  1. Play the sound
  2. Use an audio recording tool to record it
  3. Save as notification-sound.mp3 in the public folder

Alternatively, download a free notification sound from:

  • Freesound.org
  • Zapsplat
  • Mixkit
